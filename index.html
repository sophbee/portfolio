About me

Thanks for stopping by!

I recently graduated from University of Richmond's The Coding Bootcamp. Before this, I received a BA in History from Wake Forest University and worked in client-facing jobs where I was responsible for customer experiences. I learned in these roles that I was more interested in improving and creating software so I started taking courses on Codecademy whenever I had time.

I came across The Coding Bootcamp and realized that I would rather throw myself into a 6 month intensive course so I could learn the material more quickly than on my own.

During this bootcamp, I've learned a few things about myself: I am a strong problem solver and researcher, am patient, and persistent. I can't wait to keep learning and growing my knowledge database!
